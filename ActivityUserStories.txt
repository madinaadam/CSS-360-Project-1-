Turning a Page:
Sami is a student who has an Ebook and wants to turn to the next page.
She hovers mouse/finger over upper-right corner
Page icon (dog-ears or cover folds in on itself; indicating the option of turning the page.)
She clicks the upper-right corner fold to confirm wanting to shift to the next page.
The book flips to the next page.

Book Selection:
Emily is a student who wants to read a specific Ebook.
She hovers her mouse/finger over the search-bar on the top of the page.
She clicks on the search bar to confirm.
She starts to type out her search and auto generated recommendations show based-off letters of user inputs
She either clicks and confirms the book selection or enters her search.
The desired book appears (full screen) on the cover page.
